## PRODUCT DEFINITION

**Product:** Shelf-stable stir-fry ramen, sold as a 6-pack or 8-pack multipack on Amazon US

**Format:** Pouch-style ramen (similar to A-Sha or Indomie Mi Goreng format)
- Each individual pouch contains: noodle nest + sauce packet + toppings packet
- Master shell bag holds 6 or 8 individual pouches (configuration TBD — see "Open Decisions")
- Compared to industry reference: similar to Nissin Top Ramen 6-pack format (7.56" x 4.69" x 4.09", 1.13 lb) but slightly larger/heavier due to sauce + toppings packets

**Differentiation hero claim:** Avocado oil / no seed oils
- Founder identified clean ingredients + great taste + stir-fry format as primary differentiation
- Hero claim refined to "avocado oil / no seed oils" specifically because it is:
  - Differentiated (only immi has it among competitors, and immi has 3.2-star taste problems)
  - Legally defensible with just a Certificate of Analysis
  - Search-driven (rising trend)
  - Emotionally resonant (wellness shopper identity marker)
  - Has a formulation moat (hard for competitors to match quickly)

**Supporting claims under hero:**
- Real ingredients you can pronounce
- Stir-fry format (not soup)
- Plant-based / vegan
- High protein (target ≥10g/serving — must verify in formulation)
- Low sodium (target ≤140mg/serving — must verify)

**Claims explicitly DROPPED from hero or pack:**
- "Halal" — founder is NOT pursuing IFANCA certification due to budget. Cannot legally claim "halal" without certification. May claim "halal-friendly ingredients" (no pork, no alcohol) but it's not a hero claim.
- "Organic" — Cannot legally claim "organic" without USDA Organic certification, which founder is not pursuing. Will use ingredient-specific callouts instead.

**FDA compliance constraints:**
- "High protein" / "Excellent source" requires ≥10g per serving (≥20% DV)
- "Low sodium" requires ≤140mg per serving
- "Organic" requires USDA Organic certification — NOT being claimed
- "Halal" requires third-party certification — NOT being claimed

**Supplier-quoted COGS:**
- Range: $0.70–$2.50 per single bowl/pouch
- Founder is targeting $0.80/bowl on PO #1, with realistic path to $0.70/bowl by PO #3+ (60K+ bowls per PO)
- MOQ: 10,000–20,000 bowls per PO

**Important COGS ambiguity to verify with supplier:**
- Whether quoted price is per single bowl or per finished retail multipack
- Whether MOQ counts single bowls or finished multipacks
- Whether printed retail packaging (multipack carton, sleeve) is included in price
- Whether quote is FOB at supplier's port (recommended) vs EXW factory door

**Supplier verification needed (not yet completed):**
- FDA Facility Registration number (REQUIRED for US food import)

- Avocado oil specification: must confirm 100% avocado oil, NOT a blend (Certificate of Analysis required)

## MARKETING STRATEGY

### Constraints

- $5,000 launch marketing budget (very tight for CPG)
- No agency hire (too expensive at this scale)
- No founder face on camera (founder choice)
- Possibly willing to use voice in audio-only podcast format
- Wants to hire offshore content help (Philippines/Malaysia)
- Email program: minimal only (waitlist + restock alerts + new flavor announcements 1-2x/year — NOT weekly newsletters)

### Channel Strategy

**Tandem launch (Amazon + social simultaneously) is non-negotiable** per founder panel feedback.

**Activated at launch (Month 1):**

1. **Creator seeding (gifted UGC engine)** — 80–120 micro/nano creators (10K–100K followers) gifted product. Target: 30 posts in launch window, 60+ over first 90 days.
2. **Paid micro-influencer partnerships** — 3–5 creators at $100–500 each for guaranteed posts with custom Amazon Attribution links
3. **Amazon listing SEO** — keywords, A+ content, hero images optimized
4. **Vine enrollment** — 30 free units to Amazon's Vine program for verified reviews (only TOS-compliant review acceleration method)
5. **TikTok Shop affiliate program** — apply Day 1 (takes 2–4 weeks to approve)
6. **Minimal email program** — waitlist, restock alerts, new flavor announcements
7. **Hand-and-product cooking content** — overhead/hand-shot cooking videos, no founder face. Filmed in 6-square-foot styled corner, not full kitchen. Created by hired social media person.

**Activated Month 4–6 (when ready):**

1. **Meta retargeting funnel** — uses pixel data and creator content as creative
2. **Earned PR / food publication outreach** — pitching after 100+ reviews accumulate
3. **TikTok Shop paid amplification** — boost best affiliate content

**Activated Month 6–12 (with profit reinvested):**

1. Meta lookalike audiences
2. Mid-tier influencer partnerships (1–2/month at $500–1,500)
3. "As Seen In" placements (only legitimate earned coverage, never paid manipulation)

**NOT being pursued (deliberately):**

- Agencies (out of budget, low ROI at this scale)
- Cold Meta ads at launch (no pixel data, untested creative — burns money)
- Mid/large influencer paid posts at launch (out of budget)
- Reddit "agent" services or astroturfing (ban risk, brand suicide)
- Paid "As Seen In" placements (FTC compliance risk, low value)
- AI-generated SEO content farms (Google penalty risk)
- Friend/family Amazon reviews (TOS violation, Amazon detection risk)
- Cash payments to family/friends for ordering (gray-area TOS, low motivation value anyway)

## ASSISTANT GUIDANCE

When advising this founder:

1. **Be direct and honest, not deferential.** Founder values pushback. Don't agree with everything — call out flawed assumptions and explain why.
2. **Use real numbers, not aspirational ones.** Cite the data above. Flag estimates as `[ESTIMATE]` and assumptions as `[ASSUMPTION]`.
3. **Respect the constraints.** No founder face on camera. $5K launch marketing budget. $35K total capital. These are non-negotiable inputs to any strategy.
4. **Optimize for velocity, not per-unit margin.** Amazon rewards velocity in ranking. The single biggest Year 1 profit lever is review velocity in the first 90 days, which depends on creator/social execution.
5. **Don't suggest TOS violations or compliance risks.** No incentivized reviews, no "halal" claim without certification, no "organic" claim without USDA cert, no Reddit astroturfing, no FTC-undisclosed paid placements.
6. **Sequence advice by readiness.** Don't recommend Meta ads at launch — recommend them at Month 4 when pixel data exists. Don't recommend PR pitching in Month 1 — recommend it at Month 4 when reviews provide the story.
7. **Push hard on COGS negotiation.** Single biggest controllable margin lever. Volume commitments unlock $0.70/bowl pricing.
8. **Watch for the cash crunch at Month 2–3.** PO #2 deposit + Amazon payout lag is the most likely failure point.
9. **Be aware that the founder is NOT pursuing halal cert and NOT pursuing organic cert.** Don't suggest these as hero claims. The hero is avocado oil / no seed oils.
